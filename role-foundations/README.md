# Role Foundations

A Customer Success Manager in technical business to business software as a service is responsible for:

1. Onboarding and Adoption
   - Guiding customers from contract to first value.
   - Driving adoption of core and advanced features.
   - Ensuring they understand key workflows and best practices.

2. Ongoing Relationship Management
   - Regular check ins and strategic conversations.
   - Multi threading relationships across stakeholders.
   - Acting as the face of the product to the customer.

3. Risk Management and Escalations
   - Monitoring health scores and usage patterns.
   - Spotting early warning signs such as low adoption or stalled projects.
   - Coordinating with Support, Product, and Engineering on escalations.

4. Value Communication such as Executive Business Reviews or Quarterly Business Reviews
   - Turning raw usage and outcomes into executive friendly stories.
   - Highlighting wins, progress, and agreed next steps.
   - Framing the platform as a strategic asset, not just a tool.

5. Renewals and Expansion Support
   - Preparing the ground for renewals with no last minute surprises.
   - Identifying expansion opportunities aligned to customer objectives.
   - Partnering with Sales or Account Management where appropriate.

# First 90 Days Checklist

A practical roadmap for ramping as a new CSM.

## Days 1 through 30: Learn and Observe

- Understand the product deeply including core use cases and main workflows.
- Learn the most common customer personas.
- Shadow calls with experienced Customer Success Managers.
- Read internal playbooks and help center documents.
- Build your own product and persona cheat sheet.

## Days 31 through 60: Take Ownership with Guidance

- Own a smaller set of accounts or segments.
- Run your own calls with support from your manager or peers.
- Use QDIA for every call and log detailed notes.
- Start running partial Quarterly Business Reviews or sections of Quarterly Business Reviews.
- Test and refine your proactive outreach patterns.

## Days 61 through 90: Operate as a Great Customer Success Manager in Training

- Own a standard portfolio for your role level.
- Drive clear outcomes such as onboarding progression and adoption increases.
- Proactively identify at risk accounts and propose recovery plans.
- Partner with Sales on at least one renewal or expansion motion.
- Refine your personal Customer Success Manager system based on what is working.

This playbook is a living document. As you gain experience, update it with:
- Real email examples
- Account stories
- New frameworks
- Refined workflows
